# Brand Guidelines

**Department:** Marketing  
**Classification:** Internal  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective:** March 2026

## Brand voice

Our voice is **clear, confident, and human**. We avoid jargon unless speaking to a technical audience. Tone by channel:

| Channel | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Website & product | Professional, helpful, concise |
| Social media | Approachable, optimistic, on-brand |
| Sales collateral | Outcome-focused, credible, customer-centric |
| Internal comms | Direct and inclusive |

## Logo usage

- Use the **primary logo** on light backgrounds; use the **reversed logo** on dark or photo backgrounds.
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the letter **A** in the wordmark on all sides.
- Minimum display width: **120px** digital, **1 inch** print.
- Do **not** stretch, rotate, recolor, or add effects to the logo.

## Color palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary indigo | `#4F46E5` | CTAs, links, key highlights |
| Secondary violet | `#7C3AED` | Accents, gradients |
| Neutral slate | `#334155` | Body text |
| Success green | `#059669` | Positive metrics, confirmations |
| Alert amber | `#D97706` | Warnings, limited-time offers |

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Inter SemiBold or equivalent sans-serif
- **Body:** Inter Regular, 16px minimum on web
- **Line height:** 1.5 for body, 1.2 for headlines

## Imagery

- Prefer authentic photography of employees, customers, and products in real environments.
- Avoid stock photos that feel generic or off-brand.
- Illustrations should use the approved palette and rounded-corner style.

## Approval

All external-facing assets (ads, landing pages, press releases, social posts) require **Marketing review** before publish. Submit drafts in **Brand Portal** at least **3 business days** before go-live.
