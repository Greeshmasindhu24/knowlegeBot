# Marketing Campaign Approval Process

**Department:** Marketing  
**Classification:** Internal  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective:** March 2026

## Scope

This process applies to:

- Paid digital campaigns (search, social, display)
- Email nurture and product announcement sends
- Event sponsorships and webinar promotions
- Partner co-marketing materials

## Campaign tiers

| Tier | Budget (USD) | Approvers | Lead time |
|------|--------------|-----------|-----------|
| **Tier 1** | Under $5,000 | Marketing manager | 5 business days |
| **Tier 2** | $5,000 – $50,000 | Marketing director + Finance | 10 business days |
| **Tier 3** | Over $50,000 | VP Marketing + CFO | 15 business days |

## Required submission fields

1. Campaign name and objective (awareness, leads, retention)
2. Target audience and department alignment
3. Channels, creative assets, and landing page URL
4. Budget breakdown and expected ROI or KPI targets
5. Start/end dates and compliance notes (legal review if regulated industry claims)

## Workflow

1. **Draft** — Campaign owner completes the brief in **HubSpot Campaigns**.
2. **Creative review** — Brand team checks logo, copy, and accessibility (WCAG AA for web).
3. **Legal review** — Required for Tier 2+, claims about security/compliance, or customer testimonials.
4. **Approval** — Approvers sign off in HubSpot; no spend without approved status.
5. **Launch & monitor** — Owner reports weekly metrics during active flight.

## Social media posting

- Pre-schedule posts in **Sprout Social**; do not post from personal accounts for company messaging.
- Respond to public comments within **4 business hours** during business days.
- Escalate crisis or negative viral threads to **marketing-comms@acmecorp.com** immediately.

## Prohibited without executive approval

- Discounts or pricing promises not on the public price list
- Endorsements implying regulatory certification we do not hold
- Use of customer logos or quotes without a signed release
