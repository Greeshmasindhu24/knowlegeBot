# Data Privacy and Retention Policy

**Department:** Legal  
**Classification:** Restricted  
**Version:** 1.3

## Scope

This policy applies to personal data processed by Acme Corp products, corporate systems, and vendor subprocessors.

## Lawful bases

We process data based on contract performance, legitimate interest (with balancing test), legal obligation, or consent where required (marketing emails, optional analytics).

## Data subject rights

Residents of the EU, UK, and California may request:

- Access and portability (JSON export within 30 days)
- Correction of inaccurate records
- Deletion where no legal retention applies
- Restriction or objection to certain processing

Requests are handled via privacy@acmecorp.com with identity verification.

## Retention schedule

| Data type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| Customer application logs | 90 days |
| Support tickets | 3 years |
| Employee HR records | 7 years post-termination |
| Financial records | 7 years |

## Cross-border transfers

Transfers outside approved regions use Standard Contractual Clauses and supplemental measures documented in the vendor registry.

## Breach notification

Material breaches are escalated to Legal within **4 hours**; regulatory notification within **72 hours** where required.
