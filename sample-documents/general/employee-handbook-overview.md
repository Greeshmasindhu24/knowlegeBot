# Acme Corp Employee Handbook — Overview

**Effective date:** January 1, 2026  
**Owner:** People Operations  
**Classification:** Internal

## Welcome

Acme Corp provides enterprise software and professional services to global clients. This overview summarizes policies referenced in the full handbook.

## Working hours

- Standard schedule: Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM–5:30 PM local time
- Flexible start/end within ±1 hour requires manager approval
- Core collaboration hours: 10:00 AM–3:00 PM

## Benefits snapshot

| Benefit | Eligibility | Notes |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Medical / dental / vision | Full-time after 30 days | Multiple plan tiers |
| 401(k) | Full-time after 90 days | 4% company match |
| PTO | See HR leave policy | Accrual by tenure band |
| Learning stipend | All employees | $1,500 USD per calendar year |

## Workplace conduct

Employees must follow the Code of Conduct, protect confidential information, and report concerns to HR or the ethics hotline (1-800-555-0199).
