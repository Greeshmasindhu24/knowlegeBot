# Acme Corp Code of Conduct

**Classification:** Public (shareable with candidates and partners)

## Our values

1. **Integrity** — We are honest in communications, contracts, and financial reporting.
2. **Respect** — Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation are prohibited.
3. **Security** — We safeguard customer and employee data.
4. **Compliance** — We obey applicable laws in every jurisdiction where we operate.

## Conflicts of interest

Employees must disclose outside employment, board seats, or financial interests that could influence business decisions. Disclosures go to Legal via the ethics portal within 10 business days.

## Gifts and entertainment

- Employees may not accept gifts over **$100 USD** from vendors or customers without manager and Legal approval.
- Entertainment must be reasonable, infrequent, and tied to a legitimate business purpose.

## Reporting violations

Report suspected violations anonymously via the ethics hotline or to compliance@acmecorp.com. Retaliation against reporters is grounds for termination.
