# AI Response Review Guidelines (Human-in-the-Loop)

**Audience:** Reviewers  
**Classification:** Internal

## Purpose

Reviewers validate flagged copilot answers before they are cited as authoritative in regulated workflows.

## When responses are flagged

- Confidence score below team threshold
- Question touches HR termination, legal advice, or medical topics
- Retrieved sources contradict each other
- User submitted negative feedback

## Review checklist

1. **Accuracy** — Does the answer match uploaded source documents?
2. **Completeness** — Are important caveats from the source included?
3. **Citation quality** — Do citations point to the correct department-owned doc?
4. **Policy risk** — Could following this answer violate law or company policy?
5. **Tone** — Professional, no unauthorized commitments

## Outcomes

| Decision | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Approved** | No change; log notes if minor wording issue |
| **Revised** | Provide corrected answer; optionally request document reindex |
| **Rejected** | Mark as not suitable; notify user to contact subject-matter expert |

## SLAs

- P1 (payroll, safety): **4 business hours**
- P2 (general HR/IT): **1 business day**
- P3 (informational): **3 business days**

## Escalation

Escalate to Legal or HR leadership when content involves litigation, investigations, or executive compensation.
