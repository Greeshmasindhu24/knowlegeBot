# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Department:** Finance  
**Classification:** Internal  
**Effective:** March 2026

## Eligible expenses

- Business travel (air, rail, lodging, meals while traveling)
- Client entertainment pre-approved in writing
- Home office equipment up to **$500** one-time (remote employees)
- Professional certifications directly tied to current role

## Submission rules

1. Submit expenses in **Concur** within **30 days** of incurring the cost.
2. Attach itemized receipts for amounts over **$25 USD**.
3. Manager approval required before payment; Finance reviews amounts over **$1,000**.

## Travel guidelines

| Category | Policy limit |
|----------|--------------|
| Domestic airfare | Economy class; book 14+ days ahead when possible |
| Hotels | GSA per-diem rate for city |
| Rental car | Compact/intermediate; decline insurance if corporate policy covers |
| Meals (solo travel) | $75/day aggregate |

## Non-reimbursable

- Personal travel extensions, alcohol without client present, traffic fines, gym memberships.

## Payment timing

Approved reports paid within **10 business days** to payroll-linked bank account.
