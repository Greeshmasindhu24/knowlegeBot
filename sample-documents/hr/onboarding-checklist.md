# New Hire Onboarding Checklist

**Department:** Human Resources  
**Audience:** People managers and new employees  
**Classification:** Internal

## Before day 1

- [ ] IT provisions laptop, MFA token, and email
- [ ] HR sends I-9 / payroll forms via DocuSign
- [ ] Manager schedules welcome 1:1 and team intro
- [ ] Buddy assigned from same department

## Day 1

- [ ] Complete security awareness training (required within 24 hours)
- [ ] Sign NDA and acceptable-use policy
- [ ] Set up VPN, password manager, and Slack
- [ ] Review Employee Handbook overview

## Week 1

- [ ] Meet skip-level manager (30 minutes)
- [ ] Access department wiki and source repositories (Engineering only)
- [ ] Enroll in benefits within **30 days** of start date
- [ ] Complete role-specific compliance modules in LMS

## Day 30

- [ ] Manager conducts onboarding survey
- [ ] New hire presents "learned so far" summary to team
- [ ] HR confirms probation check-in scheduled for day 60
