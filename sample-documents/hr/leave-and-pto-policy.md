# Leave and Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

**Department:** Human Resources  
**Classification:** Internal  
**Version:** 2.1

## PTO accrual (US employees)

| Tenure | Annual PTO | Accrual rate |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| 0–2 years | 15 days | 1.25 days / month |
| 3–5 years | 20 days | 1.67 days / month |
| 6+ years | 25 days | 2.08 days / month |

PTO accrues on the first day of each month. Unused PTO carries over up to **5 days**; excess is paid out in Q1.

## Requesting time off

1. Submit requests in **Workday** at least **10 business days** before planned leave (24 hours for sick leave when possible).
2. Manager approval is required for blocks of 3+ consecutive days.
3. Blackout periods (year-end close, major releases) may restrict PTO — communicated by department leaders.

## Sick leave

- **10 days** per year, separate from PTO
- Doctor's note required for absences of **3+ consecutive days**

## Company holidays (2026)

New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving (2 days), Winter break (Dec 24–26).

## Parental leave

Primary caregivers: **12 weeks** paid at 100% base salary. Secondary caregivers: **4 weeks** paid. Notify HR 30 days before expected leave when feasible.
